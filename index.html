Designer's Notes
Today's news has this satisfying quality where everything is dense and consequential—$1.2 trillion, $745 million, 20,000 deaths—so I wanted the design to reflect that weight through pure typographic mass. The hero story is sized to dominate because that's genuinely the biggest number and probably the most structurally important story. Then the grid compresses everything else into uniform cells that expand when you want depth. It's basically information density made legible through hierarchy. No color, no ornament—just scale doing all the editorial work.
