Massive Winter Storm Brings Snow, Ice and Power Outages Across U.S.

ABCNEWS 900,000+ WITHOUT POWER
A massive winter storm stretching across the United States has knocked out power to over 900,000 people and is crippling travel nationwide. The storm has brought heavy snow and dangerous freezing rain, with snow totals topping 8 inches in Arkansas, 11 inches in Illinois and Ohio, 13 inches in Indiana, and significant accumulations across multiple states. Winter storm warnings are in effect for major cities including Dallas, St. Louis, Cleveland, Washington D.C., Philadelphia, and Boston. At least 3 inches of sleet have fallen in Louisiana, Mississippi, Texas and Tennessee, while strong winds pose threats to ice-coated trees and power lines.
