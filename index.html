Supreme Court to Review Video Privacy Protection Act's Consumer Definition
On January 26, 2026, the Supreme Court granted certiorari in Salazar v. Paramount Global, a case that will interpret the 1988 Video Privacy Protection Act. The case addresses a circuit split over whether the VPPA's definition of 'consumer' applies to all goods and services from video providers or only to audiovisual services. This seemingly technical question has become central to numerous recent data disclosure lawsuits against streaming platforms and could reshape how digital privacy law applies to modern entertainment companies.

Research published in Nature on January 28, 2026, projects climate change impacts on malaria distribution across Africa through mid-century.
